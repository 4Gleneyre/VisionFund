"Vision Machines"

The Vision Fund will begin an entirely new approach to the way we read and look at books. People all over the world will be empowered to relentlessly pursue God’s vision for their lives. We will do this by serving one another with LOVE and SERVICE as our core philosophy.

Our Mission

To equip the minds of children towards their God-given dream, by providing vending machines for honest, purposeful and creative books.
